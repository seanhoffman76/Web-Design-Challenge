Latitude vs. Humidity


One of the weather variables we harvested for this analysis was humidity level for the date of measurement. Based on the observations made on 2/29/2020, we cannot observe any meaningful correlation between humidity and a location's distance from the equator. Humidity levels greater than 80% could be observed at all latitudes for the cities that were part of our sample. As the cities' latitudes get nearer the equator we observe a general clustering of elevated humidity values, but there are a number of outlier values in the northern hemisphere below 20 degrees N latitude that are below 20% humidity indicating a lack of correlation between humidity and latitude.
