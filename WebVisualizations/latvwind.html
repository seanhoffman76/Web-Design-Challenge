Latitude vs. Wind Speed


One of the weather variables we harvested for this analysis was wind speed on the date of measurement. Based on the observations made, wind speed and a location's distance from the equator yielded little to no correlation. The plotted sample indicates that most locations in our representative sample experienced wind speeds below 15 MPH on February, 29 2020. This indicates that north/south location on the planet has minimal bearing on the amount of wind present at a given time.
